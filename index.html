About

Hello! I am an incoming Assistant Professor in Texas A&M University's Department of Geology and Geophysics starting August, 2025. My research focuses on sea-level change in the past and future and sea level interactions with ice sheets, coastlines, permafrost, and land hydrology. My group uses geophysical models, Bayesian statistics, fieldwork, and data compilation to disentangle solid and surface Earth processes over the Plio-Pleistocene, the 21st century, and beyond.
